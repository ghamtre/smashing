PERSON OF THE WEEK

Tracy Lee (@LadyLeet) is a JavaScript developer, Google Developers Expert, and entrepreneur who loves to build companies and communities. She co-founded the framework agnostic JavaScript mentoring and consulting firm This Dot Labs and, to report on the latest improvements in the JavaScript ecosystem, she organizes This.JavaScript.
